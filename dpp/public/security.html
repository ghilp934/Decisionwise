Security & Reliability

Built for teams that can't
afford ambiguity

Every design decision in Decisionproof starts from the assumption that your decision data is sensitive, your audit trail is load-bearing, and your payment information is not ours to store.

🗂️

Every decision is logged

All decision runs are recorded with a full input/output trace. You can export the complete audit log at any time from your dashboard — no request needed, no TTL on the data.

🔒

WORM-protected evidence storage

Audit evidence is written to a write-once, read-many (WORM) S3 bucket. Records cannot be modified or deleted after creation — not by the application, not by an operator mistake.

💳

PayPal-only payments

We do not store card numbers or payment credentials. All payment processing is handled by PayPal. Guest checkout is supported — no PayPal account required.

🔑

API key auth — no OAuth complexity

Access is controlled via API keys you create and revoke from your dashboard. Keys can be rotated instantly. No OAuth flows, no token refresh logic to manage on your side.

⚙️

Deterministic outputs

The same input always produces the same output. There is no hidden model state, no random sampling, no version drift between calls. Results are reproducible and auditable.

🏗️

Tenant data isolation

Your decision data is scoped to your account. There is no shared data layer across tenants. API keys only authorize access to your own tenant's decisions.

A note on private beta

Decisionproof is in paid private beta. We operate a production-grade infrastructure on AWS — EKS, RDS, ElastiCache, S3 — but we do not publish uptime SLAs during the beta period.

If you experience an outage or unexpected behaviour, contact us directly at support@decisionproof.io.kr. We respond within 1 business day. Refund requests within 48 hours of purchase are honoured — see our billing FAQ.

We respond to all security and compliance enquiries within 1 business day.
