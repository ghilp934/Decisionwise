FAQ

What happens after 30 days?

Your access expires. You can renew at any time from your dashboard. We'll send a reminder email before expiry.

Can I use a credit card without a PayPal account?

Eligible buyers may be able to pay by card through PayPal guest checkout. Availability depends on the checkout flow PayPal presents.

Is there a free trial?

Not at this time. During the paid private beta, access is subscription-only.

How do usage limits work?

Each 30-day cycle includes 1,000 decision credits with a hard cap of 2,000. Workspace-level rate limits also apply during beta.

How do I cancel?

Simply do not renew. Your access remains active until the 30 days expire — no action is needed to cancel.
