🛡️ Sandbox Hard Limits (enforced by the runtime)

Every Sandbox request is evaluated against the limits below before any AI inference cost is incurred. Requests that would breach any limit are rejected immediately with HTTP 429 quota-exceeded. There is no overage billing — the plan is priced as US$29 per 30-day cycle with no additional charges in any condition.

  • Per-run spend cap: US$5.00 (reservation.max_cost_usd) — maximum USD reserved for a single run. Exceeding is rejected, not truncated.
  • Workspace rate limit: 60 requests per minute (sliding window). Exceeding returns HTTP 429 with RateLimit-Remaining / Retry-After headers.
  • Monthly metered-operation cap: up to 2,000 operations per 30-day access cycle. Hard cap, fail-closed — once the cap is reached, further requests return HTTP 429 quota-exceeded until the next paid cycle.
  • Per-run execution timeout: 30 seconds. Runs that exceed this are terminated and reconciled by the reaper.
  • Per-run input / output token limits: 16,000 / 4,000 tokens. Oversized requests are rejected with HTTP 422.
  • API keys per workspace: up to 3 concurrent dp_live_* keys.
  • No overage billing: any breach yields HTTP 429, never a charge. The $29/30-day payment is the only amount Decisionproof bills for Sandbox access.

These numerics align with the internal runtime entitlement configuration (pricing_ssot.json) for the Sandbox paid private beta. They are contractual limits for the Sandbox tier only; B2B Design Partner contracts define their own limits in the signed pilot agreement.

FAQ

What happens after 30 days?

Your Sandbox access expires and runs stop being accepted. You can renew at any time from your dashboard; a reminder email is sent before expiry. For data retention: Sandbox includes Hot online access for 30 days by default. After the Hot window, Sandbox records and result artifacts are not guaranteed to remain online or retrievable and may be queued for deletion/purge under the applicable data handling policy, unless a separate plan, contract, legal hold, or security/audit obligation requires otherwise.

Can I pay by card without a PayPal account?

Card payment may be available through PayPal guest checkout for eligible buyers. Availability depends on the checkout flow PayPal presents to you — Decisionproof does not control this.

Is there a free trial?

Not during the Sandbox paid private beta. Access is subscription-only.

How do Sandbox limits work?

Sandbox is time-boxed and limit-enforced by six concrete numerics: 30-day access window, 60 req/min workspace rate limit, up to 2,000 metered operations per 30-day cycle (hard cap), per-run spend cap US$5.00 via reservation.max_cost_usd, 30 s per-run execution timeout, and 16,000/4,000 per-run input/output token limits. Exceeding any limit returns HTTP 429 quota-exceeded; there is no overage billing. The Sandbox plan is not the B2B Design Partner offer.

How do refunds work?

As a matter of commercial policy, refund requests are reviewed case-by-case within 48 hours of purchase. Outside that window, we do not offer refunds as a matter of policy. Any non-waivable consumer-protection rights you have in your jurisdiction (for example, Korean e-commerce cooling-off rights for individual consumers) continue to apply and are not limited by this policy — see the Terms of Use. Contact support with your PayPal order reference.

How do I cancel?

Simply do not renew. Access remains active until the 30 days expire; no action is needed to cancel. There is no auto-renewal to switch off.
